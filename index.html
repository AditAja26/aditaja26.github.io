November 17, 2024

Poster for industrial talk

I attended a webinar called the AirAsia Industry Talk, organized by the Faculty of Computing at UTM. The session highlighted AirAsia’s transition from a low-cost airline to a digital ecosystem leader, focusing on their digital transformation journey, early adoption of cloud technologies, and microservices architecture. It also covered recruitment opportunities in software engineering and data science, along with strategies for remote work adaptation and fostering an inclusive work culture.

Creating the poster summarizing these insights was a collaborative effort that enhanced my skills in teamwork, information analysis, and design. This experience deepened my understanding of the pivotal role of ICT in driving innovation and reinforced the importance of developing technical and soft skills for career readiness. Overall, the webinar and the poster project were not only informative but also inspiring, motivating me to explore real-world applications of technology further.

December 12, 2024

visit to utm digital

I attended a seminar at UTM Digital, the digital department of UTM Space, where we gained insights into its organizational structure and operational framework. During the visit, we explored the open office space, which showcased a collaborative and innovative work environment, and visited UTM Digital Care, the department’s customer support division. This experience provided a comprehensive understanding of how UTM Digital manages its digital services and customer engagement.

For the project, we created a video summarizing our visit and all the valuable lessons we learned, such as the importance of efficient workflows, teamwork, and customer-centric approaches in a digital ecosystem.
