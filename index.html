November 17, 2024

Poster for industrial talk

I attended a webinar called the AirAsia Industry Talk, organized by the Faculty of Computing at UTM. The session highlighted AirAsia’s transition from a low-cost airline to a digital ecosystem leader, focusing on their digital transformation journey, early adoption of cloud technologies, and microservices architecture. It also covered recruitment opportunities in software engineering and data science, along with strategies for remote work adaptation and fostering an inclusive work culture.

Creating the poster summarizing these insights was a collaborative effort that enhanced my skills in teamwork, information analysis, and design. This experience deepened my understanding of the pivotal role of ICT in driving innovation and reinforced the importance of developing technical and soft skills for career readiness. Overall, the webinar and the poster project were not only informative but also inspiring, motivating me to explore real-world applications of technology further.

January 17, 2025

ArKotak: Augmented Reality for Architecture

As part of my SECD2523 Technology & Information Systems course, my team developed ArKotak, an Augmented Reality (AR) headset and platform designed to transform architectural model visualization. This project aimed to address challenges like high costs, limited flexibility, and time constraints in traditional physical model-making by enabling students to interact with 3D designs in a virtual environment.

ArKotak allows users to upload architectural models into a Unity-based platform, visualize them in AR/VR, and manipulate designs in real-time. My role as Documentation and Communication Specialist involved Documented the process, ensuring all design thinking steps were clearly recorded. Collaborating with my team taught me the value of iterative design and user-centered problem-solving.

Key Learnings: This project deepened my understanding of AR/VR development tools like Unity, emphasized the importance of empathy in user research, and highlighted how structured teamwork drives innovation. Iterating based on user feedback was crucial in refining the prototype’s usability and functionality.
